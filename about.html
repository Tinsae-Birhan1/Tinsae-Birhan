About US

S-DAS is software development and support company dedicated to providing end to end solutions for your business and it is situated in Bole next to Sky light Hotel, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. The team consists of full-stack web and mobile developers, It HelpDesk, client support,system solution designers as well as business experts with advanced work experience.

S-DAS has two departments software development department and client support department
The software development department help enterprises convert their ideas into a state of the art products perfectly tailored to their customer needs by applying cutting-edge software development methodologies and technologies. we develop innovative and creative products and services that provide total communication and information solutions. Among a plethora of services, web design and development, applications, ERPs, ERPNEXT, CRMs, e-commerce solutions, business-to-business applications, business-to-client applications, managed hosting and internet portal management are few that we offer which are known for their security, scalability, robustness, and performance.

The client support department deliver excellent customer-centric support and service. The company provides its clients with the best of technological and operational excellence. The quality and performance-based work process and commitment to making a difference for its clients in terms of cost savings, reduction of AHT in query resolution and operational efficiency make it the ideal choice for outsourcing call center services. We provide customer support for different companies who has or is planning to launch a new system, most importantly technology related. The support we provide is classified into two. The first one is to employees of a company where new system is installed, and the second one is to users(customers) of a company and are new to use it. And we intend to serve corporate, customer-focused computer support companies.
